💡 **What:** Replaced the array mapping (`map`) and `Set` instantiation in the `longestStreak` calculation in `ProfileView.vue` with an optimized single-pass loop and fast numeric string parsing for dates. Fixed a flakey test in `useNotifications.storage.test.ts`.
